Springfield Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name is "Springfield Chamber of Commerce." This name clearly represents the city in which the chamber operates, making it recognizable to local businesses and residents.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website includes:

  • Promoting local businesses through comprehensive member services, offering tools and resources to help businesses grow and succeed in the community.
  • Serving as a central hub for community engagement, providing information on events, economic initiatives, and public policies that support local economic development and community well-being.

Color Scheme

The chosen color scheme includes:

  • Primary Color: Blue (#0044cc) for headers and accents, symbolizing trust and professionalism.
  • Secondary Color: Light Gray (#f2f2f2) for backgrounds, providing a clean and modern look.

Typography

The typography choices are:

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif, for a bold and modern appearance.
  • Body Text: 'Roboto', sans-serif, chosen for readability and a clean aesthetic.
